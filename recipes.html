Classic Taco Seasoning

This is a classic taco seasoning that I use for taco bowl meal preps:

Ingredients:1 TBPS Chili Powder,1 1/2 tsp cumin,1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper,1/2 tsp paprika (smoked is best),1/4 tsp garlic powder,1/4 tsp onion powder, 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes, 1/4 tsp dried oregano.

Instructions: Mix these ingredients into one pound of meat, ground beef/turkey or diced chicken or steak.
