For the last couple months, we've been using Datto RMM for our remote monitoring solution. They've got a built in option for Automation, which has streamlined our device deployment. We've been able to automate the installation of Teams, VPN software, Microsoft Teams and Chrome. We've also found a pre-built solution for remotely wiping PC's after user termination. We look forward to seeing how else we can automate our frequent tasks to free up more of our time for other tasks.
